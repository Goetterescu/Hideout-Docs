Tier Two

Requirements

Students are required to pass this exam if they want to earn their badge. They are allowed to have a fail rate of up to 20%. This exam has to be done with any road safe bus or small truck (no trailer allowed, the truck/bus has to be equipped with three axles, not more and not less).
The vehicle has to be equipped with all blinkers, head, rear, brake and tail lights. The vehicle has to be in perfect condition before the exam. Instructors are in charge for any accident or other incident!
