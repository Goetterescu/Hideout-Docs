Tier Three

Requirements

Students are required to pass this exam if they want to earn their badge. This exam has to be done with any road safe semi truck and trailer. The trailer and the semi truck have to be equipped with two or three axles each. The Examinator can deny the use of a certain combination.
The combination has to be equipped with all blinkers, head, rear, brake and tail lights. The combination has to be in perfect condition before the exam. All skills listed below have to be tested during the exam.
Examinators are in charge for any accidents or other incidents!
