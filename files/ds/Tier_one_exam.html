Tier One

Requirements

Students are required to pass this exam if they want to earn their badge. They are allowed to have a fail rate of up to 25%. This exam can be done with or without an trailer attached to any road safe car (no trucks and no heavy vehicles are allowed). The car is not allowed to be equipped with more than two axles, the same goes for the optional trailer.
The car and the optional trailer have to be both equipped with all blinkers, head, rear, brake and tail lights. The combination has to be in perfect condition before the exam. Instructors are in charge for any accident or other incident!
