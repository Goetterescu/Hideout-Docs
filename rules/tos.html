Terms of Service:

If you disregard any of the following rules, you will get a warning or ban (temporary or infinite, depending on the case). Multiple infractions will result in a ban.

You must be at least 13 years old to use our services, as stated by the COPPA.

Insulting

Please do not insult anyone outside of a roleplay session. You can insult someones character during a session, but don't get personal.

Singularity

One account and character per player.

Begging

Do not beg for anything, you will get what you deserve.

Backseat Moderation

Leave the real issues to the moderators, they will take care of those. It is forbidden to impersonate staff members or to moderate the server (while not being an moderator).

Unwritten

There are unwritten rules. Simply try to not do something stupid in here.

Changes

Rules might change over time, please try to stay up to date with those.

Content
